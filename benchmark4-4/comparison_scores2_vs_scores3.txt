- Total: 95/95

Conclusion: the old FileSystem losses were harness artifacts caused by comparing
models with extends-vs-in signature encodings. Applying extends_to_in.py after
Ringert detects the PrimSig/SubsetSig merge problem, then regenerating and
checking instances against the normalized models, fixes this case.
